1. We will choose a secret word/phrases.
2. The length of word/phrases is equal to number of the black line.
3. You will need to guess our word/phrases letter by letter by click in letter
   you want.
4. Start guess letters: If your guess is correct we will replace the blank line
   with the correct letter.
5. Whenever you guess a letter that is not in the secret word/phrase we will
   draw one part of hangman.
6. You will win when you guess the correct word. You will lose if the picture
   of hangman is done (6 times wrong answer -> you lose).
